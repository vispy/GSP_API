# M057-S021-CLOSEOUT-S022-OPENING - S021 closeout and S022 opening

## Mission

M057

## Goal

Close S021 with a resolver proof checklist and open a consultation-gated stage for selecting and
designing the first real remote-source family.

## Acceptance

- S021 is marked completed in Mission Control.
- A durable S021 checklist records what the resolver proof did and did not prove.
- S022 is opened as a design/consultation stage, not an implementation stage.
- Mission Control recommends a ChatGPT Pro consultation packet before real remote fetch work.
- No remote fetch, credential exchange, dynamic loading, filesystem access, or Datoviz remote-data
  requirement is added.

## Stop conditions

Stop before implementing any HTTP, object-store, cloud, Zarr/OME-Zarr, COG, map-tile, STAC, DVID,
TileDB, browser fetch, credential, redirect, DNS, cache, decoder, or Datoviz remote-data behavior.

## Source

S020/S021 stop-condition checklists, ADR-0008, P006, M055, and M056.

## Result

Completed. Added the S021 resolver proof checklist, marked S021 complete, opened S022, and recorded
the next recommendation to prepare a ChatGPT Pro consultation packet before real remote-source work.
